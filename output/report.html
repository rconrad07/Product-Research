Hypothesis: Replace Grid View with Stacked List View to reduce choice paralysis for room selection.
Ideally suited for properties with >3 room types. List views significantly reduce cognitive load when comparing complex attributes (amenities, rate rules). Aligns with industry standards (Marriott, Hilton).

Executive Summary

The hypothesis is strongly supported by user experience research and competitor analysis. While grid views offer superior initial visual appeal, list views are functionally superior for detailed comparison tasks involving price, room features, and cancellation policies. Given the user's reported "overwhelm" with 9+ options, switching to a stacked list format will align with Hick's Law (reducing decision time) and standard cognitive scanning patterns.
